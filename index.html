SAMMAAN 2020


An innovative Global Talent Search Programme in the field of Classical Music and Dance across multiple genres brings you an opportunity of being recognized among the top artists in the Indian music and dance industry, your performance critiqued by them and a scholarship to pursue higher studies in your desired field of art.

Rithwik Foundation for Performing Arts is a non-profit organization set up to promote and rejuvenate the interest in Indian classical music and performing arts particularly among the youth. We, along with the Shankar Mahadevan Academy have embarked on a Digital Global Talent Search Programme in the fields of Classical Music and Dance across multiple genres. The objective of this initiative is to create a platform to identify emerging talent globally, while integrating them with our foundation through focused mentoring by reputed Maestros to further nurture their talent.
